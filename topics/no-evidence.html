“There’s no evidence Jesus even existed”

The #1 TikTok myth that collapses in under 60 seconds.

The atheist/TikTok claim:
“Jesus is basically a myth. No real proof outside the Bible. Gospels written centuries later, full of contradictions. Just recycled pagan stories.”

1. Non-Christian sources blow the “myth” claim out of the water

Four hostile or neutral sources within 80–100 years (most ancient figures have zero):

  • Tacitus (Roman senator, AD 116) – “Christus… put to death by Pontius Pilate”
  • Josephus (Jewish historian, AD 93) – Jesus lived, called Messiah, crucified under Pilate, brother named James
  • Pliny the Younger (AD 112) – Christians in Turkey already worshipping “Christ as to a god”
  • Lucian of Samosata (~165) – mocks Christians for worshipping a “crucified sophist”

Alexander the Great has ZERO surviving sources from his own century. Jesus wins.

2. The New Testament is embarrassingly early & reliable

  • Paul quotes a resurrection creed (1 Cor 15:3-8) that dates to within 3–5 years of the cross.
  • Mark written in the 60s AD — while eyewitnesses were still alive to correct it.
  • Over 5,800 Greek manuscripts — no ancient work comes close.

3. The cultural explosion no one can explain without something real happening

  • Strict monotheistic Jews suddenly worship a crucified man as God — the ultimate blasphemy.
  • Cowardly disciples who ran away turn into fearless preachers willing to die.
  • Church starts in Jerusalem weeks later — worst possible place to fake a resurrection (just show the body!).

4. The “Failed Messiah” argument—no one would invent this on purpose

If you were inventing a religion in 1st-century Israel, you would make it popular, powerful, and attractive.
You would NOT invent the one story guaranteed to get you hated, mocked, and executed.

A made-up Messiah rides in on a war horse and kicks Rome out.
He does NOT get stripped naked, crucified, and cry “My God, why have you forsaken me?”

The only reason the disciples preached this scandalous version is because it actually happened and they couldn’t deny it.

Key verses to drop in the group chat

  • 1 Corinthians 15:3-8 – Paul lists eyewitnesses still alive
  • Acts 2:32 – Peter preaches the resurrection in Jerusalem 50 days later
  • Acts 26:26 – Paul to King Agrippa: “This was not done in a corner”
Bottom line your kid can fire back:
“More evidence for Jesus than almost anyone from antiquity. The real question isn’t ‘Did He exist?’ — it’s ‘Why did thousands start worshipping a crucified criminal as God within months if nothing happened?’”
