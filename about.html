About Faithproof

Faithproof equips the next generation with honest answers to the hardest objections — so their faith stands firm when the world pushes back.

Most kids first meet the strongest arguments against Christianity alone on their phone at 2 a.m. — and it shakes them.

We believe it’s far better to bring those objections out into the light early, in a safe place, surrounded by truth and people who love them.

Every lesson is free, printable, and made for families, youth groups, and homeschoolers.
